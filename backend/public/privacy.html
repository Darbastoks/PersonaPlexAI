Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 28, 2026

1. Introduction

Welcome to ChatVora ("we," "our," or "us"). We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring you have a positive experience when using our AI-powered customer engagement tools.

This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect information when you use our website, dashboard, and AI widget services.

2. Information We Collect

We collect information at two levels: from our direct customers (clients) and from the users who interact with our clients' AI widgets (end-users).

  • Client Information: When you sign up for ChatVora, we collect your business name, email, industry, and payment details (processed securely via Stripe).
  • End-User Information (Leads): When users interact with a ChatVora widget, our AI may collect names, email addresses, phone numbers, and specific inquiry details to provide results to the client.
  • Technical Data: We may collect standard log files, IP addresses, and device information to maintain service security and performance.

3. How We Use Information

We use information only to fulfill our service obligations:

  • To provide and maintain our AI chatbot services.
  • To capture and transmit leads directly to our clients.
  • To process payments and manage subscriptions.
  • To improve AI response accuracy through secure processing (via Groq and Deepgram APIs).

Important: ChatVora does NOT use your captured lead data for our own marketing purposes, nor do we sell lead information to third parties.

4. Data Storage & Retention

All data is stored securely on MongoDB Atlas (partitioned across global AWS/GCP regions). We respect the following retention schedule:

  • Lead Data: Stored securely for the duration of a client's active subscription.
  • Post-Cancellation: Lead data is automatically deleted 12 months after a subscription ends, unless a manual deletion is requested earlier.

5. Third-Party Services

We utilize trusted third-party providers to deliver our service. These providers have their own privacy policies governing their data handling:

  • Stripe: Payment processing and billing.
  • Deepgram: Neural text-to-speech (TTS) and voice processing.
  • Groq: Large Language Model (LLM) processing for chat intelligence.
  • MongoDB Atlas: Secure cloud database storage.

6. Your Rights (GDPR & CCPA)

Depending on your location, you have specific rights regarding your data, including:

  • The right to access and receive a copy of your data.
  • The right to request the deletion of your data.
  • The right to withdraw consent for data processing at any time.

To exercise these rights, please contact us at the email address provided below.
